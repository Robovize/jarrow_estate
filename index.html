Why have a property in Jarrow

Shopping

Jarrow is home to the Viking Shopping Centre, shopping destination with a 400 free parking spaces. With over 50 shops you can visit butchers, bakers and main street stores.

Transport

Jarrow is reached from the south by the A1(M) via the A194, and is connected to North Tyneside and Northumberland via the Tyne Tunnel. Jarrow has three Metro stations: Jarrow, Bede and Fellgate. You can even walk under the Tyne River through the pedestrial tunnel.

Outdoor spaces

Jarrow is green place with many parks and recreation areas including West Park, Charlies Park and Jarrow Skate Park.
